About Us

About Us

Nepal humanitarian association was founded in 2009 and administrated by a Board of Trustees who have dedicated themselves to the noble task of uplifting the quality and standard of the lives of others.

Our Mission

In Nepal, one of the most desperate countries for education and training, our activities have started in 2009 in the capital, Kathmandu. In order to provide high-quality education for the children of the country in the region, the Trust has purchased 1037 square meter of land with the help of Nepali benefactors in the city of Kathmandu. In 2012, a building was constructed which has been serving as a student dormitory. At present, 83 students from the level of secondary and high school are educated./p>
